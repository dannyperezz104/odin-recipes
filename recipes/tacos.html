Tacos

Description

Al pastor tacos are made with al pastor pork cooked on a trombo.

Once the tortillas and the meat are hot enough, the chef swiftly slices

a pineapple slice on top of it and leaves it up to you to add salsa, cilantro, lime, and onions to the top!

    Ingredients:
  • 2 lbs pork shoulder, sliced
  • Adobo sauce ingredients
  • 16 small corn tortillas
  • Toppings: pineapple, cilantro, onions, lime
  • Salsa
  • Optional: cotija cheese, avocado
    Steps:
  1. Prepare Adobo Sauce
  2. Marinate Pork
  3. Cook Al Pastor
  4. Assemble Tacos
  5. Serve and Enjoy
