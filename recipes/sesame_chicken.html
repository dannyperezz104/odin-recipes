Sesame Chicken

Sesame Chicken is a classic for any Chinese food enthusiast

Its bite sized, but the toastiness of the sesame seeds add so much more.

You gotta try it!

    Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 2 green onions, chopped (for garnish)

    Steps:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger
  2. Toss chicken in cornstarch until coated
  3. Heat a pan, cook chicken until browned and cooked through
  4. Pour sauce over chicken, stirring to coat
  5. Sprinkle sesame seeds and green onions on top
  6. Serve over rice and enjoy!
