Caramelized Pear Pizza

My first time trying pear pizza was at Moon Palace, a resort in Cancun.

I have loved it since and have tried another delicious pear pizza on Amsterdam, New York.

Just get your cheese pizza base, and get ready to pear-ify it!

    Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 2 ripe pears, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup caramel sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • Fresh arugula for topping

    Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (232°C)
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface
  3. Spread caramel sauce over the dough
  4. Layer sliced pears, mozzarella, blue cheese, and walnuts
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until crust is golden
  6. Top with fresh arugula before serving
