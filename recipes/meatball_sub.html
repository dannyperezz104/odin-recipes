Meatball Sub

Description

Prepare a homeade hearty sandwich filled with tender, flavorful meatballs simmered in marinara sauce, topped with melted mozzarella or provolone cheese, all nestled in a toasted hoagie roll. It’s a classic comfort food that’s saucy, cheesy, and perfect for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsely
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1/2 tbsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Hoagie rolls or sub rolls
  • sliced mozzarella or provolone cheese
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Make the Meatballs - In a large bowl, combine ground beef, Italian sausage, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, egg, minced garlic, chopped parsley, salt, pepper, and oregano. Mix until well combined, then shape into small meatballs.
  2. Cook the meaballs - Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add meatballs and cook until browned on all sides. Remove excess grease if necessary.
  3. Simmer in Sauce - Pour marinara sauce into the skillet with the meatballs. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, allowing the meatballs to cook through and absorb the flavors of the sauce.
  4. Assemble the subs - Preheat your oven to broil. Split hoagie rolls and place a few meatballs with sauce inside each roll.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and enjoy your homemade meatball sub!
